Join the Windmill Primary School Oxford 100 Club today. Your monthly contribution directly supports our school's library and projects, while giving you a chance to win in our regular prize draws.

What is it?

The Windmill Primary School 100 Club is a private lottery for families, staff and friends of the school. The Club forms part of the school community and is one of the ways in which the Windmill Parent Teacher Association (PTA) raises funds.

How it works

Members purchase a numbered ticket (from 1 to 100), which enters them into the monthly prize draws. The monthly draws are overseen by the school Headteacher.

The lottery funds

Half of the monthly income goes directly towards funding our projects, such as supporting the library and the other half is returned as cash prizes for our lucky winners.

The prizes

Every month members of the 100 Club have the chance to win £25 for 1st place, £15 for 2nd place and £10 for 3rd place.

How to Get Involved

3

Win!

Wait for our monthly draws. Winners are contacted directly and announced on this website.
